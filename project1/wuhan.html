Wuhan, China

My hometown Wuhan is a city in central China.

It's a huge metropolis but not famous as Beijing or Shanghai.

The Wuchang uprising in 1911 is Wuhan's most remarkable event in Chinese history.

The revolution successfully ended China's five thousand years of the imperial rule system.

Bridges & Water

Since Wuhan is divided by rivers and lakes, there are many bridges in the city.

The first bridge across the Yangtze is built-in here in 1955, this bridge is the symbol of the city.

Visit

Unlike other Chinese cities, Wuhan is not famous for the fancy shopping malls and expensive restaurants.

Due to its location in central China, Wuhan combined the style of Northern and Southern China.

Welcome to Wuhan if you have a chance to visit China. Enjoy the delicious food and the atmosphere of common people.
